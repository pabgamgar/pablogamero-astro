Submoney is a minimal subscription tracker focused on a single question: how much money should be in your bank account today to cover every upcoming charge. It keeps the interface light and practical:

- Calendar view of upcoming charges
- Reminders on payment day and beforehand
- Yearly spend snapshot per subscription
- Mark-as-paid flow for irregular recurring payments
- Trial tracking so you can decide before it converts

The goal is to keep your subscription buffer clear and avoid surprise overdrafts.
